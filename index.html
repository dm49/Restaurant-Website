Robo Burgers History

Robo Burgers was created by Trax. While working at E.V.I.L Labs Trax volunteered to cook for the annual picnic. It was here that Trax first discovered his passion for burgers. Using science and his skills as a lab assistant Trax developed his patented mix of spices that gives our food the flavor you can only get at Robo Burgers.

Robo Burgers uses the finest genetically modified and laboratory grown ingredients.
